Why you need Day 1

Human resource processes can be tedious and chaotic if done manually. Nowadays, automation has become essential in effectively managing these day-to-day processes . Day 1 is a complete hr process solution that allows companies to centralize all these processes. From employee data, attendance, performance, expenses, health and safety, and HR requests, we’ve got you covered.

EXPLORE OUR CAPABILITIES

Features

Employee Data
Management

Record and store employee data that can be generated into printable reports

Attendance Management

Syncronization of data from the biometrics. This feature also allows employees to request leaves and overtime subject to approval. Attendace summary is viewed by the superior and approved by the HR

Performance Management

Allows employees to plan their deliverables, log their work progress, and reflect back at the end of the week/month. KPI records are displayed allowing supervisors to monitor/rate employee performance

HR Service Request

When in need of assistance, employees can create tickets with prioritization levels (major/minor). Admins can easily view and manage tickets notifying the employee of the movement and status of the ticket

Health and Safety Management

Allows HR to easily monitor health status of employees via health checklist automatically sent to employees as part of safety management

Expense and Bills Management

Allows employees to create expense reports for reimbursement requisitions to be reviewed and approved. This can be integrated with the accounting system used by the company
